Privacy & Policy

Privacy

Privacy Policy

How Instant Munchkin handles your information — short version: barely any of it.

Starter template. Review and adapt with a professional before publishing, and replace the contact address below.

1. The short version

Instant Munchkin is a companion app for the Munchkin card game. It works without an account and is designed to keep data on your device. We don't sell your data, and we don't want more of it than we need to run the app.

2. What we collect

  • Game data — player names you enter, levels, gear and scores. This is stored to run your session and, when you share a session, to sync it to other players' devices.
  • Basic technical data — anonymous diagnostics and crash information that help us fix bugs and keep the app stable.

We don't ask for your real name, email, contacts, photos or location.

3. How we use it

Solely to provide and improve the app: run your game, sync shared sessions between devices, and diagnose problems. We do not use your data for advertising profiles.

4. Sharing

Session data is shared only with the players you choose to share a game with. We don't share personal data with third parties except service providers that help operate the app (for example, crash reporting), under appropriate safeguards.

5. Your choices

You can clear game data at any time from within the app. Because there's no account, removing the app removes the data stored on your device.

6. Children

Instant Munchkin is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date below.

8. Contact

Questions? Email hello@instantmunchkin.com.
